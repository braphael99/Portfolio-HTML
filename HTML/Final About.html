About Kitchen Records

We were started in early 2017 to give a platform to up and coming artists and producers. We have had success so far with our current artists, and we hope to continue the tradition.

Location

We are located in Briargate, Colorado Springs, Colorado. We have one location and one studio but are hoping to expand to a larger area in the upcoming future.
